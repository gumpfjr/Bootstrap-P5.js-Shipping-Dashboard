About our Company

Ferryboat Shippers was established in 1997 in Seattle, Washington. The company started out as a small family owned package delivery system, which traveled mainly by the seattle ferryboat system. The orders were sent in via mail and then shipped through the ferry by our delivery people.

This system continued and grew for 10 years and mainly delivered to the Seattle city region. In 2007 our company went fully online and packages were no longer delivered on foot by our package delivery people, but by transit via trucks. This new found system allowed for quicker and more efficient delivery of packages in the Seattle area.

As our company has grown over the years we no use the ferry system in the Seattle area, but it will always be the symbol of where we started. We now have over a dozen packing facilities that deliver to locations all over the U.S. in different states.

Our company has served our loyal customers since the beginning and we will continue to do so for generations to come!
